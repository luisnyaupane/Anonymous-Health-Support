🕯️ Suicidal Thoughts

Get help to calm your thoughts, feel heard, and find support — anonymously and safely.

💡 What to Do in Emergency Situations

  • Call 1166 for mental health emergency (Nepal).
  • Call 100 for police rescue or physical danger.
  • Stay near others or public spaces if you're feeling unsafe.
  • Use our Emergency Help button to alert our response system.
  • If someone else is in danger, try to talk calmly and get them to this page or a helpline.
  • Do not hesitate — your safety is worth it.

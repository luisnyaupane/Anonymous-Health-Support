🧘 How to Handle Suicidal Thoughts

  • Pause. Breathe deeply. Try to stay in the present moment.
  • Tell yourself: "These feelings will pass."
  • Talk to someone — you don't have to fix it alone.
  • Write a letter to yourself. Reflect. Let the pain out safely.
  • Try to delay actions by 24 hours — the urge often passes.

🌱 Seeing Life Differently

It’s okay to feel lost. But you are still here — and that means there is possibility. Here are small ways to shift your view:

  • Start a small routine. Tea in the morning. A walk. Music.
  • Notice something beautiful every day — a sky, a child’s laugh.
  • Set one small goal, like brushing your teeth or opening the window.
  • Let go of what hurt you — and forgive yourself.

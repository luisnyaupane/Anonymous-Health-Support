🛡️ Abuse Support & Recovery

This portal is for anyone experiencing or witnessing abuse. You can learn about your rights and forward moving approaches.

⚖️ Learn Your Rights

  • You have the right to live without violence — even from family.
  • Emotional, financial, or cyber abuse is illegal under Nepali law.
  • Women can access legal and financial aid even without husband's permission.
Read Nepali Law

🚶 What You Can Do

  • Reach out anonymously to NGOs or our team — we'll protect you.
  • Use a safe contact name and upload any threats or messages.
  • Ask for help relocating if home is unsafe.
  • Prepare silently: hide copies of your citizenship, finances, etc.

🌿 Stories of Recovery

Women in Nepal who escaped abuse have found safety, independence, and work with help from INGOs.

  • One mother started her own tailoring shop after support from SAATHI.
  • Another survivor was trained and employed as a peer support worker.

🧍‍♀️ If You Witness Abuse

If you witness abuse, contact one of these parties for help:

  • 📞 100 – Nepal Police: For immediate rescue.
  • 📞 1145 – National Women Commission: For reporting abuse and getting support.
  • 📞 1660-01-50000 – SAATHI: Domestic violence and women's protection.
  • 📞 01-5551991 – WOREC: Legal and psychosocial support.
  • 📞 9851286771 – Cyber Bureau: Report blackmail, threats, or online abuse.

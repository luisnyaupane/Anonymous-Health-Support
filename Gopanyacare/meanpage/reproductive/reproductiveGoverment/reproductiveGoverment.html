🏛 Government Efforts in Reproductive Health

Programs & initiatives by Nepal Government to support safe and accessible reproductive care.

🍼 Aama Surakshya Program

Free delivery services and incentives for institutional births. Pregnant women receive up to Rs. 3,000 based on region.

🩺 Free Family Planning Services

Includes condoms, pills, implants, IUDs, vasectomy, and minilap — available at public hospitals and health posts across Nepal.

🚺 Free Sanitary Pad Distribution

Public schools provide free pads for girls in grades 6–12. Part of menstrual hygiene promotion effort.

🏥 Adolescent-Friendly Services (AFHS)

Designated youth-friendly corners in health posts offering counseling, reproductive info, STI treatment, and referrals.

📞 1115 Reproductive Health Helpline

Call anonymously for questions on menstruation, family planning, STIs, or pregnancy. Run by the Ministry of Health.

📋 Safe Abortion Services

Legal up to 12 weeks on request, 18 weeks in special cases. Available at certified government & NGO facilities.

ℹ️ Contact your nearest health post or visit fwd.gov.np for more info.
